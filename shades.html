Shades. Not Your Mother’s Shades.

Today’s shades are beautiful and are no longer relegated to the garage! They’re still great for protection from too much sun, heat or cold. But today, they can display your flair for decorating with numerous styles and hundreds of fabrics, colors and patterns from which to choose. You can specify continuous loop or cordless, whichever suits your need! You can also opt for motorized shades, which can be integrated into your “Smart House” systems!

Cellular Shades

Cellular or Honeycomb shades are a great choice for lots of reasons. Light filtering is great for common rooms, while room darkening is the choice for bedrooms and media rooms. And cell shades have insulating properties that help rooms stay cooler in the summer and warmer in the winter – helping to keep energy costs in check.

Sunscreening Shades

Sleek, smart, stylish. Available in roman or roller versions, sunscreening or solar shades allow you to enjoy the outdoors, cutting the sun’s glare without blocking your view. These shades are perfect for reducing glare on computer or television screens, or for sun protection on hardwood floors and furniture. Solar shades allow much needed relief from heat and sunlight during the warmer months and are essential additions to your sunroom or screen porch.

Roller Shades

Today’s roller shades are great looking, functional, and durable. With so many possibilities to choose from – fabrics, patterns, styles and colors, you’ll be amazed at what you can do with roller shades.

Roman Shades

Looking for a classic or contemporary touch? Roman shades are it. The beautiful look of draped fabric adds elegance to your room. And they’re available in numerous styles to meet your decorating needs and can be customized with optional fringe, tassels and more.

Sheer Shades

Elegant, beautiful, versatile. Sheer shades add a distinctive and elegant look to your home. An innovative product that combines the beauty of a sheer with functionality similar to a blind, sheer shades help block harmful UV rays and gently filter sunlight to cast a soft, rich glow throughout your room. When you want to enjoy your view, they can be rolled into the headrail, and Voilá, they are gone!

Woven Wood Shades

Natural and renewable bamboo, grasses and reeds are woven into hundreds of beautiful colors and patterns. Woven woods bring the outside in, adding a classic, yet contemporary look to your home. Enjoy them unlined for gentle light filtering or lined for extra privacy and light control. Many customers enjoy them in combination with draperies for a layered look.

Shade Options

Shades offer many options—cordless, top-down-bottom-up, motorization, cassettes / facias / valances, privacy liners, decorative beading or trim and much more. Your sales representative can help you select the features and options that are right for you.
