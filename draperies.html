Exquisite Custom Draperies

With all of the beautiful choices in textiles today, why not choose draperies? Our representatives can bring our beautiful swatch books right to your home, so that you can pick fabrics that go best with your décor in its actual setting.

Draperies or other soft treatments add ambience to any room and can be made in seemingly endless styles. In addition, full-length, lined draperies can help you save on energy costs by blocking drafts and retaining heat.

COM (Customer's Own Material)

Though we have a very extensive collection of fabrics from which to choose, you can also supply your own fabric and we will make your window treatments and accessories with fabrics that you have found.

Before purchasing your fabric, please ask your sales rep what information he or she needs in order to estimate the yardage that you should purchase. They may require information on the pattern repeat, fabric content, strength, and thickness in order to make your draperies and accesories fit the fabric that you have chosen.

Accessories

We also offer beautiful hardware to further customize the "look” you want to achieve. Then, to make things even easier, we will install your new draperies — all you have to do is to stand back and enjoy!
