Free In-Home Estimates!

(919) 878-0006

Q

What products does all-phase offer?

A

We carry everything you need to make your windows look beautiful — blinds, plantation shutters, honeycomb shades, roller shades, woven woods, roman shades, draperies and more.

Q

Do you offer financing?

A

Yes we do! We offer six months interest-free financing to qualifying purchasers. Your representative can review the details with you.

Q

What brands does All-Phase carry?

A

We carry the largest selection of brands in the region including names like Graber, Comfortex, Norman, Alta, Vista and many more. We also offer our WoodMaster brand, our own line of custom window treatments, for added value and quality.

Q

What about warranties?

A

All our products are backed by the manufacturer’s limited lifetime warranty for defects. In addition, we offer free service calls during the first year you own your product to ensure they are functioning properly and to make adjustments as necessary. Ask your representative for warranty details on specific products.

Q

What can I expect from an in-home consultation, and is there a fee?

A

Our in-home consultations are free* and take approximately one to two hours depending upon the number of windows and the type and number of products. Your representative will initially discuss with you the products you are interested in, custom measure all windows to ensure a perfect fit, show you extensive samples and provide you with a detailed, written estimate.

Q

Do you have a showroom?

A

Yes, our showroom is conveniently located off I-540, just minutes away from Triangle Town Center. See the “Contact” section of our site for details and directions. Since our appointments are scheduled in the field, please call ahead to let us know you are coming, so we can be sure to have a sales representative available to help you.

Q

What payment options do you offer?

A

As is customary in the industry, we require a 50% deposit at the time you place your order. You may pay via check, or major credit card. The remaining balance of 50% is due at the completion of your installation.

* Drapery appointments require a $75 fee for a two-hour consultation. The consultation fee will then be applied to the cost of the drapes upon ordering from us.
