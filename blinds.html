Blinds: Versatile. Durable. Beautiful. Affordable.

Blinds are a design element that help complete the look and feel of your room. They're practical — letting the sun shine in on cool days or blocking heat and light during the summer. All-Phase Blinds & Shutters even offers in-home consultations to check out the excellent blind choices we offer to Raleigh, Durham, Cary, Chapel Hill & Wake Forest residents. The choices are almost limitless - materials, colors, options and more!

Free in-home estimate.
Let's get started!

Faux Wood Blinds

Offer the look of wood, without the cost. Great for the whole house or high moisture areas like kitchens, bathrooms and garages. Available in a wide variety of stain colors and textures.

Wood Blinds

Offer a timeless, classic look with many paint and stain colors from which to choose. From bright whites to rich stains, wood blinds effortlessly match your decorating style.

Composite Blinds

Combine the look of wood with the benefits of synthetic materials.

Aluminum Blinds

Feature a retro look and style in hundreds of stunning colors. Aluminum blinds are amazingly light-weight, yet still sturdy. They are ideal for contemporary interiors, commercial spaces, and rental properties.

Vertical Blinds

Large windows or sliding glass doors aren’t a challenge. Vertical blinds provide a stylish and affordable solution to covering large expanses – choose from wood, vinyl or fabric options.

Blind Options

Valances

Add the finishing touch to your blinds, covering the head rail. Choose classic or contemporary designs.

Wand or Cord Controls

Standard blinds come with cord lift and tilt controls. Wand tilts are an additional option.

Cordless Lift

For a cleaner look, as well as added safety, add the cordless feature to your window treatments!

Routless

Provides increased privacy and light control, while making slat removal easier for cleaning.

Cloth Tapes

Make a bold statement by accessorizing with cloth tapes. Choose from dozens of colors and patterns.

Motorization

Simplify your life with one-touch control to tilt your blinds.
