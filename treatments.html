What window treatment is right for you?

Please look over the list below prior to booking your appointment and let us know your thoughts, so that your salesperson can bring the samples you most want to see! Please remember that your window treatments will be with you for many years, so now is the time to specify the treatment that works best for you. The result will be high-quality window treatments that are customized to reflect your specific needs, choices and sense of style!

Getting treatment in your windows - fast!

Choose wood or faux blinds, mini-blinds, verticals or roller shades. These custom products can be manufactured and installed, generally within 2-3 weeks.

Energy Efficiency

Choose honeycomb shades or draperies to insulate your windows and keep energy costs down.

Environmental

Choose renewable products! Wood shutters, wood blinds or woven wood shades are made from woods, bamboos and grasses that can be re-grown and replenished. Woven woods, in particular, bring nature right into your home and add style to any window. They are available in literally hundreds of patterns and look great alone, or in combination with draperies.

Budget

Choose faux wood blinds, mini-blinds, verticals or roller shades.

Privacy

Choose shutters, blinds, honeycomb shades, woven woods with privacy liners, soft blinds, roman shades or draperies.

Decor

If you are most concerned with how your treatments will look in your home, specify virtually any product, traditional or contemporary. Your choices are almost endless-- plantation shutters, blinds with decorative options, soft blinds, woven wood shades, honeycomb/pleated/ roller or roman shadesâ€”each product will add unique beauty and elegance to your home.

Room Darkening

Choose blackout pleated, honeycomb, roller or woven wood shades in many stunning colors and fabrics. Shutters, Roman shades and draperies are also excellent choices for room-darkening. Some soft blinds have room-darkening properties, although soft blinds will not darken a room as much as the window treatments mentioned above.

Safety

If you have small children or pets, and cords are a concern, consider wand, cordless or remote options that can be added to many of the treatments above.

We hope the above list is helpful in considering your options. Please call with your thoughts and concerns prior to your appointment so that we can bring the samples that best fill your needs!
