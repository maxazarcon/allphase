Blinds

Blinds are a design element that help complete the look and feel of your room. They're practical — letting the sun shine in on cool days or blocking heat and light during the summer. All-Phase Blinds & Shutters even offers in-home consultations to check out the excellent blind choices we offer to Raleigh, Durham, Cary, Chapel Hill & Wake Forest residents.

Shutters

With their clean lines, shutters can take any window from ordinary to extraordinary. Choose from classic wood or contemporary synthetic materials to match your room-by-room requirements. And no matter the size or shape of your window, there’s a shutter solution that’s right for you.

Our Diamond Series shutters are a zero maintenance product backed by an unprecedented lifetime warranty on finish and components. You will not find a higher quality product!

Shades

Shades have come a long way in just a few years. They’re still great for protection from too much sun, heat or cold. But now they can display your flair for decorating with numerous styles and hundreds of fabrics, colors and patterns from which to choose!

Draperies

With all of the beautiful choices in textiles today, why not choose draperies? Our representatives can bring our beautiful swatch books right to your home, so that you can pick fabrics that go best with your décor in its actual setting.

Draperies or other soft treatments add ambience to any room and can be made in seemingly endless styles. In addition, full-length, lined draperies can help you save on energy costs by blocking drafts and retaining heat.

Exteriors

Make your outdoor living space even more beautiful, functional and comfortable with a wide range of exterior shade and shutter solutions.
