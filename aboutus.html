About Us
All-Phase Blinds & Shutters has served thousands and thousands of customers in the Triangle region for over 25 years. We are locally-owned and operated, with a highly experienced staff of window consultants, installers, and operations professionals.

* For further information on our location, or to contact us, please see our appointment page.

We Offer Name Brands
All-Phase offers the widest selection of window treatment brands in the Triangle from major manufactureres like Graber, Comfortex, Norman, Alta, Vista, and more. We also offer our own line of custom window treatments for blinds, plantation shutters, and shades - built to our own specifications to bring you added value and quality.
Our Mission
Our goal is to help you enhance the look of your home with beautiful window treatments at an affordable price, accompanied by a satisfying purchase and installation experience.
